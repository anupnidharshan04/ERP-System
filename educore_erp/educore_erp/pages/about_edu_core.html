Our Story: From Classroom Frustration to Global Solution

EduCore was born from a simple observation: educators were spending more time managing systems than educating students. Our founders, former teachers and school administrators, experienced firsthand the frustration of juggling multiple disconnected platforms, endless paperwork, and communication gaps that hindered educational excellence.

In 2019, we set out to create something different—not just another school management system, but an intelligent ecosystem that would seamlessly connect every aspect of educational administration. We envisioned technology that would be so intuitive and powerful that it would become the invisible backbone enabling extraordinary educational experiences.

Today, EduCore serves over 500 schools worldwide, impacting more than 2 million students and their families. But our mission remains unchanged: to empower educators by simplifying complexity and creating more time for what truly matters—inspiring young minds.
